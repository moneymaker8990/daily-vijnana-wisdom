## App Store Description

### Short Description (80 characters)
Daily wisdom teachings, meditation timer, journaling, and dream interpretation.

### Full Description

Discover the timeless wisdom of humanity's greatest spiritual traditions with MindVanta - your personal sanctuary for mindfulness, reflection, and inner growth.

**DAILY WISDOM**
Begin each day with profound teachings from the world's sacred texts:
• Bhagavad Gita - Ancient Hindu philosophy on duty and devotion
• Tao Te Ching - Lao Tzu's guide to living in harmony
• Dhammapada - Buddha's path to enlightenment
• Meditations - Marcus Aurelius' Stoic reflections
• And many more sacred texts from diverse traditions

**MEDITATION TIMER**
Cultivate inner peace with our customizable meditation timer:
• Beautiful Tibetan singing bowl sounds
• Adjustable session lengths
• Start and end bells
• Track your meditation practice

**PERSONAL JOURNAL**
Reflect and grow with guided journaling:
• Daily prompts for deeper self-discovery
• Voice journaling option
• Gratitude tracking
• Mood monitoring
• Tag and search your entries

**DREAM JOURNAL**
Unlock the wisdom of your dreams:
• Record dreams upon waking
• AI-powered dream interpretation
• Discover symbolic meanings
• Track recurring themes

**STUDY COURSES**
Deepen your understanding with structured learning paths:
• Introduction to meditation practices
• Comparative wisdom traditions
• Philosophy and ethics
• Practical mindfulness applications

**SACRED LIBRARY**
Explore a curated collection of spiritual texts:
• Full texts with verse-by-verse navigation
• Historical context and introductions
• Bookmark and favorite passages
• Offline reading

**FEATURES**
✓ Completely free to use
✓ Works offline
✓ Private and secure - your data stays on your device
✓ Optional cloud sync across devices
✓ Daily notification reminders
✓ Beautiful, distraction-free design
✓ Dark theme for comfortable reading

Whether you're beginning your spiritual journey or deepening an existing practice, MindVanta offers the tools and wisdom to support your path to inner peace and self-discovery.
