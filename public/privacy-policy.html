Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 19, 2026

MindVanta ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Journal Entries: Personal reflections, thoughts, and gratitudes you write in the journal feature
  • Dream Journal: Dream descriptions and any interpretations you save
  • Favorites: Wisdom texts and verses you bookmark
  • Study Progress: Your progress through courses and reading materials
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication

Automatically Collected Information

  • App Preferences: Settings like notification times, text size, and sound preferences
  • Usage Data: Which features you use and how often (collected anonymously)

How We Store Your Data

Local Storage: By default, all your personal data (journal entries, dreams, favorites, progress) is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

Cloud Sync (Optional): If you create an account and enable cloud sync, your data is securely stored in our cloud database (powered by Supabase) to allow access across multiple devices. Cloud data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app's functionality
  • To sync your data across devices (if you opt in)
  • To generate AI-powered dream interpretations (processed securely, not stored)
  • To send you daily wisdom notifications (if enabled)
  • To improve the app based on anonymous usage patterns

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your journal entries, dreams, and personal reflections are private and belong to you.

We may share anonymized, aggregated data for analytics purposes, but this data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For authentication and cloud data storage (optional)
  • AI Services: For dream interpretation features (data is processed but not stored)

Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete the app or clear app data. Cloud-synced data is retained until you delete your account or request data deletion.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Export your data
  • Delete your data and account
  • Opt out of cloud sync at any time
  • Disable notifications

Children's Privacy

MindVanta is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
