Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Daily Vijnana Wisdom ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Daily Vijnana Wisdom collects minimal data to provide our services:

  • Local Data: Your reading progress, favorites, and dream journal entries are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it.
  • Dream Interpretations: When you request an AI interpretation, your dream content is sent to our secure servers for processing. This data is not stored after the interpretation is generated.
  • Subscription Information: If you subscribe to Premium, payment processing is handled by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android). We do not have access to your payment details.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide personalized daily wisdom content
  • To generate AI-powered dream interpretations
  • To remember your reading progress and preferences
  • To process subscription purchases

Data Storage

All your personal data (favorites, dream journal, progress) is stored locally on your device using standard browser/app storage. We do not upload or sync this data to any external servers.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic Claude AI: For dream interpretation (dream content is processed but not stored)
  • Apple App Store / Google Play: For subscription management
  • Vercel: For web hosting

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. All communications with our servers use HTTPS encryption.

Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all your local data by clearing the app's storage
  • Use the app without an internet connection (except for AI features)
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through your device's settings

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@vijnana.app
