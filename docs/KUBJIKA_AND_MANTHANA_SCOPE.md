# Kubjika And Manthana Scope

## Kubjikamatatantra

App label: Guided Study with Selected Mindvanta Renderings.

Mindvanta includes conceptual orientation and selected non-ritual draft renderings for Kubjika, crookedness, Kaula/kula, body as mandala, Shakti, embodiment, subtle-body symbolism, and sacred power without sensationalism.

Mindvanta does not claim a complete Kubjikamatatantra translation. It does not publish ritual procedures, mantra instructions, initiation details, or lineage-restricted material.

## Manthanabhairava Tantra

App label: Advanced Guided Study Map.

Mindvanta includes an orientation map for churning, fierce awareness, Kubjika-related goddess currents, hidden power, transformation, and responsible advanced study.

Mindvanta does not claim selected renderings or a full verse-by-verse translation for Manthanabhairava Tantra.
